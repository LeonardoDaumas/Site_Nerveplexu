"Dear Colleagues and Friends, I am pleased to welcome you all to the 1st Joint FLANC and EANS Theoretical&Practical Course on Peripheral Nerve & Brachial Plexus Surgery in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, from March 31st to April 2nd, 2023.

The Course aims to increase the number of surgeons dedicated to peripheral nerve surgery. The FLANC and EANS will organize this Course for the first time as a joint venture in cooperation with the Federal University of the State of Rio de Janeiro (UNIRIO) and Hospital Universitário Gaffrée e Guinle (HUGG).

The previous editions of the Course were successfully organized by the WFNS Peripheral Nerve Surgery Committee in Leon, Spain (2016), Belgrade, Serbia (2017), Frankfurt, Germany (2018) and Buenos Aires, Argentina (2019).

Traditionally, the Course features a rich and dynamic program with a comprehensive multidisciplinary approach, covering a range from basic to innovative aspects of peripheral nerve and brachial plexus surgery in a stimulating way. The faculty members are over forty world’s leading experts from various pertinent disciplines.

It is my great privilege and honor to invite you to participate in this Course, discover and enjoy the beautiful city of Rio de Janeiro, and experience and share noteworthy scientific and social moments.

I am confident that this event will open new perspectives and result in a memorable mind and soul-enriching interprofessional and interpersonal exchange.

We look forward to welcoming you to Rio de Janeiro for another great Theoretical & Practical International Course in Peripheral Nerve & Brachial Plexus Surgery!"

DR. MED. CHRISTIAN HEINEN
